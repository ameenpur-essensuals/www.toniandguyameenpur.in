Manicure

Our manicure services are designed to give your hands a refreshing and elegant look. We offer a variety of options including classic, gel, and spa manicures. Our classic manicure includes nail shaping, cuticle care, a soothing hand massage, and your choice of nail polish. For longer-lasting results, our gel manicure uses high-quality gel polish that cures under UV light, providing a durable and chip-resistant finish. For a truly indulgent experience, try our spa manicure, which includes exfoliation, a hydrating mask, and a relaxing massage to leave your hands feeling soft and rejuvenated. All our manicures use premium products to ensure your nails are healthy and beautiful.

Pedicure

Treat your feet to the ultimate pampering experience with our pedicure services. We offer classic, gel, and spa pedicures to meet your specific needs. Our classic pedicure includes nail trimming, cuticle care, a foot scrub, a relaxing massage, and polish application. The gel pedicure offers the same benefits as the classic but with the added advantage of long-lasting gel polish. For those looking for a more luxurious experience, our spa pedicure includes a foot soak, exfoliation, callus removal, a hydrating mask, and an extended foot and leg massage. We use high-quality products to ensure your feet are not only beautiful but also well-cared for.

Facials

Hair Smoothing

Our hair smoothing treatments are designed to give you sleek, manageable hair. We offer a range of options including keratin treatment, Brazilian blowout, and smoothening treatments. Keratin treatments infuse natural keratin into the hair shaft, eliminating frizz and adding shine. This treatment is ideal for all hair types and lasts for several months with proper care. The Brazilian blowout is another popular smoothing treatment that creates a protective layer around each strand of hair, smoothing the cuticle and eliminating frizz. Our smoothening treatments are perfect for those looking for a more permanent solution to unruly hair, offering long-lasting smoothness and shine. Our professionals will assess your hair type and condition to recommend the best treatment for you.

Botox for Hair

Botox for hair is an advanced treatment that repairs and revitalizes damaged hair. Unlike traditional Botox, this treatment does not involve injections. Instead, a special formula containing proteins, vitamins, and amino acids is applied to the hair, filling in gaps and breakages in the hair shaft. This treatment restores hair's elasticity and shine, making it look fuller and healthier. It's ideal for hair that has been damaged by heat styling, coloring, or environmental factors. Hair Botox provides deep conditioning, reduces frizz, and improves overall hair texture, leaving you with smooth, shiny, and manageable hair.

Hair Straightening

Our hair straightening services offer both temporary and permanent solutions to achieve sleek, straight hair. We provide options like temporary flat ironing, permanent straightening, and Japanese straightening. For a quick transformation, our flat ironing service offers straight, smooth hair that lasts until your next wash. For a more permanent solution, we offer chemical straightening treatments that alter the hair's structure to keep it straight for months. Japanese straightening is a specialized treatment that permanently straightens curly or wavy hair, leaving it silky and smooth. This process involves applying a special solution to break down the hair's natural bonds, followed by heat treatment to reshape the hair structure. Our experienced stylists will guide you through the process, ensuring your hair remains healthy and vibrant.

Keratin Treatment

Keratin treatments are a popular choice for achieving smooth, frizz-free hair. This treatment involves applying a keratin solution to the hair, which is then sealed in with heat. The keratin penetrates the hair shaft, repairing damage and locking in moisture, resulting in shiny, healthy-looking hair. This treatment is suitable for all hair types, including curly, wavy, and color-treated hair. Keratin treatments help to reduce styling time, as hair becomes more manageable and easier to straighten. The effects can last for several months with proper care, and the treatment can be customized to achieve your desired level of smoothness and volume. At Toni&Guy Essensuals Ameenpur, we use high-quality keratin products to ensure the best results and maintain the health of your hair.
